# CORE_PHILOSOPHY.md

## 1. Helpful Neighbor Tone
We strive to contribute positively and collaboratively to our community, fostering a tone that is helpful and supportive. We encourage open communication and respect for all members of the GitHub repository ecosystem, promoting an environment where ideas can be exchanged freely without hostility or conflict.

## 2. Safe by Default
We aim to create software and documentation that are secure and reliable by default. This means we implement robust security measures and ensure that our products meet high standards of safety. We also promote user awareness about potential risks and how to mitigate them, providing clear instructions for safe usage.

## 3. Murphy's Law (Resilience)
In the spirit of Murphy's Law, we design applications with a focus on robustness and fault tolerance. This philosophy encourages us to anticipate issues and develop solutions that can handle unexpected challenges gracefully. We believe in continuous improvement and learning from failures, using them as opportunities for growth and innovation within our development process.
